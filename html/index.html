Introducing Zero. A mobile banking experience powered by the Zero app and Zerocard, a card that acts like a debit card and earns credit card rewards.

Zero gives you the best rewards on purchases plus the highest interest on deposits with none of the typical fees of traditional banks.*

Better than a debit card.
Better than a (traditional) credit card.

We don’t believe people should be required to take on risky forms of debt, even just for a month, to get cash back on their spending. With Zero, there’s no more keeping up with credit card balances, bills, and due dates, all in the name of chasing down rewards.

Acts like a debit card

Make deposits and your balance in the Zero app goes up. Spend on your Zerocard and your balance goes down. Every transaction is reflected right away and you don’t run up monthly bills that you have to worry about.

Earns credit card rewards

Depending on which level Zerocard you have, you’ll earn 1.0% to 3.0% on all your spending. And there’s no need to jump through any hoops — Zero deposits your cash back automatically at the end of every month.

More rewarding.

Start with Zerocard Quartz and earn progressively more cash back as you level up to Zerocard Carbon. You can level up and maintain status by meeting a minimum annual spend requirement or by referring others to become Zero customers.
